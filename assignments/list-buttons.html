Taxation Policy

In a market economy, any state makes extensive use of taxation policy as a certain regulator of the impact on the adverse events of the market. Taxes, as well as the whole tax system, are a powerful tool to manage the economy under market conditions. As an instrument of redistribution, taxes should extinguish the arising failures of the distribution system and encourage people in the development of some form of activity.
