Enjoy the Uzbek cuisine- a GOURMET PARADISE

by Usmanova Visolakhon

Talking about Uzbek cuisine, you can only swallow plentiful saliva, because names of these delicious dishes - pilaf, manti, shurpa, kebab, lagman, samsa - blow with such a magical aroma that it is simply unimaginable to remain calm. I want to immediately try all this, enjoying the extraordinary taste ... Uzbek cuisine is loved and revered throughout the world. It is the brightest and most varied cuisine in the East in terms of taste and external qualities.Many Uzbek recipes have a long history, and cooking is accompanied by various rituals that have survived to this day. Over a thousand-year history, Uzbek cuisine has developed its own specific features.
