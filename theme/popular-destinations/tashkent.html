Tashkent City

Tashkent is a modern metropolis- the capital of the independent Republic of Uzbekistan. The city is located in the north-east of the country, in the fertile oasis of Chirchiq river valley, 1300 feet above sea level. This city combines modernity and antiquity. Tashkent is considered as the biggest business and cultural center of the region.

Independence Square is found in the center of Tashkent city. It is a favorite resting place for Tashkent residents. This was greatly facilitated by the fact that it is the place where the city’s most beautiful fountains are located.

Climate of Tashkent

The climate of Tashkent is temperate-continental, usualy warm. It will have all four seasons. The average temperature ranges from 2.5 °C (37 °F) in January to 27.5 °C (81.5 °F) in July.

Places to Visit

The history of Tashkent has more than 2200 years, during which glorious path has been traveled from a small village to one of the largest and most beatiful cities of Central Asia. There a many beautiful places in Tashkent to visit

Hazrati-Imam Square

The Hazrati-Imam Square is located in historical part of the city-Sebzar. It is a complex of majestic historical and architechtural monuments of spiritual culture of Uzbekistan. Accorting to scholars, in the early Middle Ages, most likely, it was the center of the city, which was called Shash in those days.

Kukeldash Madrassah

Kukeldash Madrassah, the most well known historical monument in Tashkent, is an amazing example of oriental architecture, one of the characteristic structures of Chorsu artitechtural ensemble, the old-town part of Tashkent. It was built in the 16th century by the order of Uzbek Shaybanid Sultans- Barak Khan and Dervish Khan. For many centuries, the madrasah was the focus of urban life, at different times it was served as an educational institution, caravanserai and even a defensive fortress.

Amir Temur Square

Amir Temur Square in Tashkent is the unofficial center of the city, an odious and iconic place. It was named after Amir Temur, the commander and founder of huge medieval empire, began as early as XIX century. The sqaure represented a small park in center of the ciy and surrounded by Amir Temur Museum, by building of women and men's gymnasium, normal school and state bank
